Hi!

My name is Emily Nguyen! I am currently living in the East Village in NYC and attending New York University where I am participating in the Liberal Studies core and transitioning to Integrated Design and Media under the Tandon School of Engineering in my junior year. I am originally from Orange County, California; specifically, Little Saigon, where most Vietnamese-Americans reside. A lot of my academic work for Liberal Studies explores immigration and the Vietnamese-American identity; but, outside of that, I work on website and application building using Figma and Visual Studio Code.

In my free time I like to play guitar, listen to music, and learn new languages.
